Biography

The Red Army is one of two teams that has been in the league every single year since its inception. Despite his tenured service, he has still yet to achieve a championship, let alone a playoff win. In the early years of the league, The Red Army acredits their lack of success to "not caring." In recent years, his lack of success is seen as a mystery. Despite always having one of the top drafts, The Red Army always seems to underperform.

In the 2019 season, The Red Army made a dramtic name change and released an iconic video to go along with it. In this video, The Red Army bashed the RFL and its commissioner for the removal of Tyler Ulmschneider and his Cookie Charizards. Furthermore, he acknowledged that Tyler himself is the true fantasy god and would change his own team name to "Cookie Charizards" to honor him. In the video he also uttered the infamous line "Gruden has no plan," a bash at Michael Boswell's Gruden's Plan. Despite his devotion to Tyler, Brian would learn that he was a false idol as he would suffer his worst year in franchise historty up until that point.

Leading up to the 2020 season, Brian would reject Tyler but his skeptism for the league and its commissioner only grew. This led to another video that acted as a manifesto. Here The Red Army called for the members of the league to unite and reject the league's commissoner Jeremy Harley. He paints the commissioner as a fascist leader that acts in his own self-interest and cares not about the common league member. He also calls for the league to adopt a communist governing structure where no person has any more power over another. To that effect, he also acknowldeged the "father of communism" Karl Marx as his new fanatsy god and he would adopt the new team name "The Red Army". His efforts, however, came up short as the commissioner still reigns to this day. Furthermore, The Red Army would suffer their worst finsih in franchise history as they would attend back to back Panzerkampfwagen Bowls and this time finish in last place. His poor finish forced him to endure a cruel punishment put on by the other league members as each member was able to throw a pie in the face of The Red Army's owner, Brian Levesque. We have not heard any further word since The Red Army suffered this embarrassing punishment.
