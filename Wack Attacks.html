Biography

The Wack Attacks was allowed entry into the league following the RFL's most extensive search for a new manager conducted thus far. Owner Zach Zalovick had never been associated with the league prior to his entry, but he was good friends with congressman Michael Boswell Jr., which allowed him to have the chance to interview. After a last-minute cancellation by Adam Fenton for his interview, Zach was given the green light to be interviewed. That interview was conducted on that same day and Zach had left a wedding early and delayed his little boo thang just to have a chance to be in this league. He had crushed his interview and made a very strong impression on the RFL and its commissioner.
